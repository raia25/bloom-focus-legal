Privacy Policy for Focus Garden

Effective date: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Focus Garden works without account registration.

Data stored on your device

  • focus sessions (duration, completion time),
  • in-app progress (plants, coins, streaks, goals),
  • reminder preference settings.

This data is stored locally on your device to provide app functionality.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app requests notification permission and schedules local notifications on your device.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Analytics and Tracking

Focus Garden does not use third-party tracking for advertising.

4. Children’s Privacy

Focus Garden does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Retention

App progress remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in app/device settings.
  • You can remove app data by uninstalling the app.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date reflects the latest version.
